# EduAIPlatform Policies (Phase 1)

## Data classification
- Assignments: CONFIDENTIAL
- Grades: CONFIDENTIAL
- Profiles: INTERNAL
- Logs: INTERNAL (no raw assignment content)

## Retention
- Assignments auto-delete after ASSIGNMENT_RETENTION_DAYS
- AI artifacts auto-delete after AI_ARTIFACT_RETENTION_DAYS

## AI boundaries
- No external browsing
- No cross-user access
- Stateless requests
- No training on user data
